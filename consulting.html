Consulting Services

Practical consulting support for Nevada community associations and management companies focused on process, communication, and long-term sustainability.

Where We Help

Every association is different. We focus on the areas that most directly impact your owners, your board, and your management workflow.

  • Operational reviews and process mapping
  • Budgeting and reserve-related communication strategies
  • Policy and procedure implementation (non-legal)
  • Transition planning for new boards, managers, or vendors
  • Owner communication and expectation-setting strategies

How a Typical Engagement Works

  1. Initial discovery – understanding your challenges, history, and objectives.
  2. Document and process review – looking at how things are currently done in practice.
  3. Recommendations – clear, prioritized steps your team can realistically implement.
  4. Implementation support – optional follow-up to help keep things moving.

We aim to complement, not replace, your existing professional team. Legal questions are always referred to your association’s counsel, and tax or audit questions are referred to your accounting professionals.

Types of Clients

  • Community associations seeking help with specific problem areas
  • Management companies wanting a fresh look at their workflows
  • Boards preparing for transitions, growth, or difficult conversations with owners

Engagement Styles

  • Short, focused projects (single issue or community)
  • Broader portfolio reviews for management companies
  • Ongoing advisory support on a scheduled basis

What We Emphasize

  • Clarity and transparency in communication
  • Practical, implementable recommendations
  • Respect for your association’s governing documents and professionals
