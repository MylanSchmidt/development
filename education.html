Education for Boards & Managers

Focused training for Nevada community association boards and management teams, built around real-world questions and day-to-day challenges.

Why Education Matters

Volunteer board members are asked to make complex decisions about budgets, reserves, enforcement, and communication. Managers juggle multiple communities and expectations. Clear, practical education helps everyone work from the same playbook and reduces confusion and conflict.

Training Options

  • Board orientations / bootcamps for new or transitioning boards.
  • Topic-specific sessions (reserves, budgeting, financials, meetings, etc.).
  • Manager workshops focusing on process, communication, and coordination with vendors.
  • Custom Q&A sessions tailored to the unique needs of your community or portfolio.

Format & Delivery

We can deliver education in several formats to fit your schedule and audience:

  • In-person trainings for boards and managers
  • Virtual sessions via video conference
  • Blended approaches with pre-work plus live Q&A
  • Written guides and take-home reference materials

Content is designed to be practical and accessible, while respecting the roles of your existing legal and accounting advisors. We focus on process, communication, and best practices, not legal opinions.

Ideal For

  • HOA and condo boards needing a common understanding
  • Newly elected or appointed board members
  • Management companies onboarding staff or refreshing training

Popular Topics

  • Understanding budgets and reserves
  • Running effective (and shorter) board meetings
  • Working with professional vendors and counsel
  • Building transparent communication with owners
