Reserve Studies

Practical, board-friendly reserve studies and updates designed for Nevada community associations and management companies.

What We Deliver

NV Summit Services provides reserve studies that give boards clear, actionable information about the long-term repair and replacement needs of their common elements. Our reports are structured to be easily understood by both experienced managers and volunteer board members.

  • Component inventory and condition assessment
  • Remaining useful life and replacement cost estimates
  • Funding plan options and contribution recommendations
  • Charts and summaries that are easy to present to owners
  • Updates and follow-up support as your community’s needs change

Study Types & Updates

We work with communities at different stages and budgets. Whether you need a full baseline study or a focused update, we’ll help you choose the appropriate level.

  • Full / Baseline studies – comprehensive review and documentation of all components.
  • Level 1–4 style updates – ranging from off-site updates using prior data to more detailed on-site reviews.
  • Annual reviews – keeping the study current as projects are completed and costs change.

How We Work With You

Our approach is collaborative. We coordinate with community managers, board members, and other trusted professionals to understand your association’s goals, past projects, and any constraints that might affect funding decisions.

  • Pre-study call to understand your priorities and history
  • On-site or virtual walkthroughs, as appropriate
  • Draft review with management/board for questions and adjustments
  • Final report in a format suitable for board meetings and owner communication

NV Summit Services does not provide legal or tax advice. We work alongside your legal and accounting professionals so your board can make informed decisions based on a complete picture of the association’s needs.

Is This Service For You?

  • HOAs and condo associations in Nevada
  • Management companies seeking a trusted reserve study partner
  • Boards wanting clearer reports and communication tools

Credentials

Licensed Community Association Manager (CAM #226)
Licensed Reserve Specialist (RS #321)
